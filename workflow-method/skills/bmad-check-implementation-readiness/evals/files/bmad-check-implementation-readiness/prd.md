# PRD — PetClinic Scheduler

## Vision

Let pet owners book, reschedule, and cancel veterinary appointments online, and
let clinic staff manage the daily schedule.

## Functional Requirements

- **FR-1** A pet owner can register an account and add one or more pets.
- **FR-2** A pet owner can book an appointment for a pet in an available time
  slot.
- **FR-3** A pet owner can reschedule or cancel an existing appointment.
- **FR-4** A pet owner receives an email confirmation when an appointment is
  booked, rescheduled, or cancelled.
- **FR-5** Clinic staff can view and adjust the day's schedule from a staff
  dashboard.

## Non-Functional Requirements

- **NFR-1** The booking page must load in under 2 seconds on a 3G connection.
- **NFR-2** All personal pet-owner data must be encrypted at rest.

## MVP Scope

In scope: FR-1 through FR-5.

Out of scope: payment processing; SMS reminders.

## Non-Goals

- Not a veterinary medical-records system.
