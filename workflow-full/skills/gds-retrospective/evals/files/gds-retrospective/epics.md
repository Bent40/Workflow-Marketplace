# EmberFall - Epic Breakdown (excerpt)

## Epic 1: Combat & Room Flow

**Epic Goal:** Deliver the moment-to-moment loop of clearing rooms and routing through
a floor: combat unlocks doors, the player chooses where to go, and a boss gate caps the
floor.

**Success Criteria:**
- A room's doors unlock only after every enemy is defeated, with a clear cue.
- The player can see adjacent room types and pick a door, and the unchosen options are
  discarded for that floor.
- The boss door appears only after the floor's required rooms are cleared, and the
  combat loop holds 60 FPS with up to 30 active enemies (NFR-1).
