# Release Notes — v2.4

The new sync engine will processes changes in the background and it handles conflicts automatically. Users who have alot of devices will benefit from this, becuase their data stays consistant across all of them.

We have also fixed a issue where the app would sometimes freezes on startup. This was effecting a small number of users but we take stability very serious.

In order to enable the feature, navigate to Settings and then click on the Sync tab and then toggle the switch to the on position and then your devices will begin syncing.

The team are excited to ship this release. Their is more coming soon.
