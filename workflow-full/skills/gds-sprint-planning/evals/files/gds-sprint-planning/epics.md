# EmberFall - Epic Breakdown

## Requirements Inventory

### Functional Requirements
- FR-1 Room clear and door unlock
- FR-2 Branching room choice
- FR-3 Relic pickup and stacking
- FR-4 Permadeath and run reset
- FR-5 Meta-currency spend in Hub
- FR-6 Boss gate per floor

## Epic List
1. Epic 1: Combat & Room Flow
2. Epic 2: Relics & Progression
3. Epic 3: Permadeath & Hub Meta

## Epic 1: Combat & Room Flow

### Story 1.1: Clear a room to unlock doors
As a player, I want a room's doors to unlock when I defeat every enemy, so that
clearing combat is what lets me advance.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
**Given** a room with enemies, **When** the last enemy dies, **Then** all of that
room's doors unlock with an audible and visual cue.

### Story 1.2: Choose the next room
As a player, I want to see each adjacent room's type before I pick a door, so that I
can route my run deliberately.

### Story 1.3: Boss gate per floor
As a player, I want the boss door to appear only after I clear the floor's required
rooms, so that the boss caps the floor.

## Epic 2: Relics & Progression

### Story 2.1: Pick up a relic
As a player, I want to collect a relic from a treasure room, so that my build grows
during the run.

### Story 2.2: Stack duplicate relics
As a player, I want a second copy of a relic to stack additively, so that doubling
down on a relic is rewarded.

### Story 2.3: View my relic loadout
As a player, I want to see my active relics on the pause screen, so that I understand
my current build.

## Epic 3: Permadeath & Hub Meta

### Story 3.1: End a run on death
As a player, I want a run to end and reset when my health hits zero, so that
permadeath has real stakes.

### Story 3.2: Bank Embers across runs
As a player, I want my collected Embers to survive into the next run, so that failed
runs still make progress.

### Story 3.3: Spend Embers at the Forge
As a player, I want to spend banked Embers in the Hub to unlock a starting relic, so
that meta-progression rewards repeated play.
