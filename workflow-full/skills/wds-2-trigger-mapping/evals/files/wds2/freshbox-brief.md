# FreshBox — Product Brief (excerpt for Trigger Mapping)

## Vision
A credible, chef-led weekly meal-kit web presence that turns busy households into loyal weekly subscribers and answers their questions before they have to email.

## Positioning
A locally-sourced, smaller-menu, real-chef alternative to mass-market meal-kit services. Easy skip/pause with no penalty is the headline differentiator.

## Business Model
Weekly per-box subscription. The owner is at kitchen capacity and prioritizes steady, retainable subscribers over explosive growth.

## Target Users (raw notes from discovery)
- **Busy parents (primary)**: Two-income households, ages 30-45, can cook but have no time to plan or shop. They want predictability and the safety of being able to skip a week when life gets hectic. They fear locking into a rigid plan they can't escape, and fear wasted food / wasted money.
- **Health-conscious solo professionals (secondary)**: Want fresh, portion-controlled meals without grocery trips. Care about ingredient quality and sourcing. Fear over-processed "convenience food" and hidden costs.
- **Gift-givers / parents-of-students (tertiary)**: Want to set up an easy, caring food solution for someone else (e.g. a kid at university). Fear it being complicated to set up or hard to cancel.

## Success Criteria
- Increase weekly active subscribers and 8-week retention.
- Reduce repetitive support emails ("how does it work", "can I skip a week").

## Constraints
- Launch English-only, one market, small budget, web-first (no native app for v1).
