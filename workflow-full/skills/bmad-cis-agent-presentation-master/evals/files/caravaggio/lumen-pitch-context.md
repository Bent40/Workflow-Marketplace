# Lumen — Context for an Investor Pitch Deck

## Company
Lumen is a B2B SaaS that helps mid-size warehouses cut energy bills by using their
existing smart-meter and HVAC/lighting data to automatically schedule equipment around
real-time electricity prices and occupancy.

## The problem
Warehouses run lighting, HVAC, and charging on dumb fixed schedules. Electricity prices
swing wildly hour to hour, and a lot of energy is spent heating/cooling/lighting empty
zones. Energy is a top-3 operating cost and it's largely unmanaged.

## The solution
Lumen connects (read-only) to the building's existing systems, learns usage and occupancy
patterns, and shifts flexible loads to cheaper hours and away from empty zones — no new
hardware required. Operators see savings and can set comfort/safety guardrails.

## Traction (real numbers to use)
- 9 paying warehouses live.
- Average 18% reduction in energy spend in the first 90 days.
- $240k ARR, growing ~22% month over month for the last 4 months.
- 2 signed LOIs with a regional logistics chain (60+ buildings).

## Market
- ~18,000 mid-size warehouses in the target region; energy spend is large and rising.
- Comparable point solutions exist but require new hardware; Lumen's edge is software-only
  on existing systems.

## The ask
- Raising a $4M seed to expand the sales team and harden the integrations.
- Use of funds: ~50% go-to-market, ~35% engineering/integrations, ~15% ops.

## Tone / audience
- Audience: seed-stage climate/B2B investors.
- Should feel credible and data-forward, not hypey. The numbers are the story.
