# FreshBox — Scenario 01, Step 01.2: "How It Works" page

**Persona:** Busy Parent (PRIMARY)
**Scenario:** Becoming a weekly subscriber (linear sunshine path)
**This step's purpose:** Reassure a skeptical busy parent that the service is predictable and easy to escape, so they proceed to plan selection.

## Entry context
User arrived from the landing page hero CTA "See how it works". They are on mobile, evaluating during a lunch break, mildly skeptical of meal-kit lock-in.

## Driving forces in play (from trigger map)
- **Hope:** predictable weekly dinners without planning/shopping.
- **Worry:** getting locked into a rigid plan they can't escape; wasting food/money.

## What this page must achieve
- Explain the weekly cycle in 3-4 simple steps.
- Make penalty-free skip/pause unmistakable and visible BEFORE signup.
- Show a clear "what a box contains" example.
- Lead the user onward to choose a plan.

## Exit action
User taps "Choose a plan" and proceeds to the plan-selection page.

## Constraints
English-only, mobile-first viewport (390px), brand feel: warm, trustworthy, chef-credible.
