# FreshBox — Trigger Map (excerpt for Scenarios)

## Business Goals
- **PRIMARY (THE ENGINE):** Convert visiting busy parents into retained weekly subscribers (target: +25% 8-week retention).
- Secondary: Reduce repetitive "how does it work / can I skip a week" support emails.

## Personas
### Busy Parents — PRIMARY
Two-income household, ages 30-45, can cook but no time to plan/shop.
**Wants:** predictability of weekly dinners; the safety of penalty-free skip/pause.
**Fears:** being locked into a rigid plan they can't escape; wasting food and money.
**FreshBox answers:** transparent "how it works" up front; one-tap skip/pause clearly shown before signup.

### Health-Conscious Solo Professional — SECONDARY
Wants fresh portion-controlled meals without grocery trips.
**Wants:** ingredient quality, clear sourcing.
**Fears:** over-processed food; hidden costs.

## Constraints
English-only, web-first, single market, v1.
