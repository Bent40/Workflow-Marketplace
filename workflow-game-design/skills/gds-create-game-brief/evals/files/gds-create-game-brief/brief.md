# Game Brief: Tidewright

## Core Concept
A lighthouse-keeping game on a shifting coastline. You repair the lighthouse by day and
guide ships through fog by night.

## Core Fantasy
You are the steady light that keeps a small coastal community alive against the sea.

## Pillars
- A tactile, tense night beam-aiming phase (not idle).
- Day and night feed each other — last night's wrecks are today's problems.
- A small, named cast of townsfolk you get to know.

## Target Audience
Everyone who likes games. Casual and hardcore players alike will enjoy it.

## Differentiation
It's better than other cozy games because it's more fun and more polished.

## Platforms
PC and Switch, single-player.

## Comparables
Spiritfarer; Papers, Please.
