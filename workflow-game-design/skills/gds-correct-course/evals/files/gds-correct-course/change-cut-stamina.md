# Triggering Change — Cut Stamina

**Discovered during:** open playtest, sprint 4.

**Issue:** Across 30 playtesters, the stamina mechanic consistently kills the fun. Players report that running out of stamina mid-exchange feels like being randomly disarmed, not like a tactical risk. The "winded" state is the single most-complained-about moment. Spacing-to-regen makes fights feel passive instead of aggressive.

**Desired change:** Remove the stamina system entirely. Attacks and dashes should have no resource cost. Rebalance combat to gate aggression through attack startup/recovery frames and the parry system instead of through a stamina economy. We want fights to stay aggressive and readable without the stamina layer.

**Implication the team flagged:** This touches a core pillar (Risk-reward stamina), the core loop, the entire Stamina epic, the parry refund mechanic, and the HUD. We need to understand the full blast radius before we commit.
