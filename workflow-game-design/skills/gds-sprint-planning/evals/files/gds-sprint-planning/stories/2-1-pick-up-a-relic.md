# Story 2.1: Pick up a relic

Status: ready-for-dev

## Story
As a player, I want to collect a relic from a treasure room, so that my build grows
during the run.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Entering a treasure room presents a relic the player can pick up.
2. Picking up the relic applies its passive modifier for the remainder of the run.
3. The picked-up relic appears in the active loadout.

## Tasks / Subtasks
- [ ] Spawn a relic pedestal in treasure rooms (AC: 1)
- [ ] Apply relic modifier on pickup (AC: 2)
- [ ] Add relic to loadout state (AC: 3)
