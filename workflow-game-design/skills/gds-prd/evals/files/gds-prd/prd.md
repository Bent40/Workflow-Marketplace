# PRD: EmberFall

## 1. Vision
A single-player top-down action-roguelite for PC where short, lethal seeded runs
reward incremental mastery of dodge-and-strike combat.

## 2. Target Players
PC players who enjoy skill-expressive roguelites and short play sessions.

## 4. Features

### 4.1 Combat & Room Flow
#### FR-1: Room clear unlocks doors
A room's doors stay locked until all enemies are defeated.

#### FR-2: Branching room choice
The player picks the next room from 2-3 visible options.

### 4.2 Relics
#### FR-3: Relic stacking
Duplicate relics stack additively, capped at five.

## 7. Success Metrics
- SM-1: Day-1 players complete at least one full run.

## 8. Open Questions
- How is run determinism preserved across a quit-and-resume? Not yet decided.
