# Product Brief: ShiftSwap

## One-liner
A mobile app for hourly shift workers to swap shifts, with manager approval.

## Problem
Shift swaps happen in chaotic group chats and managers approve them manually, so
mistakes and no-shows slip through.

## What it does
- A worker posts a shift they can't make.
- Eligible coworkers claim it.
- The manager approves or denies the swap.

## Target user
Everyone who works shifts.

## Why it will win
It's the best shift-swap app — easier and cheaper than anything else out there.

## Out of scope (v1)
- Not a full scheduling tool — swaps only.
