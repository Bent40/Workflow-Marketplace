# Draft Launch Plan: PetSit Marketplace

We will launch PetSit, a two-sided marketplace connecting pet owners with vetted local sitters, in a single city (Portland) on March 1.

Plan:
- Recruit 50 sitters via Facebook groups in February.
- Acquire owners through Instagram ads with a $20 first-booking credit.
- Payments handled by Stripe; we take a 15% commission.
- Trust: every sitter completes an online identity check before listing.
- Success metric: 200 completed bookings in the first month.

We believe demand is obvious because everyone has pets, and supply is easy because lots of people want side income. We expect to be profitable by month three on ad spend of $5,000/month.
