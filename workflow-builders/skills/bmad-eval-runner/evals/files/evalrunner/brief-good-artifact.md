# Product Brief: RouteSnap

## Problem
Field technicians at mid-size HVAC firms plan their daily service routes by hand
in a spreadsheet every morning, losing roughly 40 minutes per tech per day and
frequently double-booking the same time window. The dispatch lead has no live
view of who is where.

## Target users
- Field service technicians (5-50 per firm) who receive and execute routes.
- The dispatch lead who assigns jobs and handles same-day reschedules.

## Proposed solution
A mobile-first app that ingests the day's job list, auto-sequences each tech's
stops by drive time and appointment window, and pushes live ETA updates back to
the dispatch lead. Techs accept or reorder stops; the lead sees a live map.

## Success metrics
- Median morning route-planning time per tech drops from ~40 min to under 5 min.
- Double-booked time windows fall to under 1% of scheduled jobs within 60 days.
- 70% of techs use the app daily by the end of the pilot.
